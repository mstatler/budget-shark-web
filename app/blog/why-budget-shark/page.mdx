# Why Budget Shark Exists

Spreadsheets sprawl. Versions multiply. Numbers drift.

Budget Shark exists to lock your budgeting and forecasting into clean,
predictable workflows — with uploads, validation, promotion, and reporting
built in so finance teams don’t live in 14 different Excel files.
